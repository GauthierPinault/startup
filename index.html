Who are we?

We are a young start-up, eager to provide services and products that will help people achieve their environmental goals.

Our goal

We believe that a transition to an environmentally responsible business model is possible and we encourage our customers to participate. As a company with a mission, we are committed to this cause.

Our services and products

Composting Service

A service of food waste collection from households and businesses and transformation into compost. This compost can then be sold to local farms and gardeners as a natural fertilizer. This service helps reduce the amount of organic waste sent to landfills and promote sustainable agriculture.

Organic Garden Kit

An organic garden kit that includes everything a person needs to start a garden, such as seeds, soil, and gardening tools. This helps people grow their own produce at home, reducing their carbon footprint by reducing the need to transport produce from distant locations.

Sustainable Cooking Class

To complement the composting and gardening services, the company also offers sustainable cooking classes that teach people how to cook using locally sourced, seasonal ingredients.
